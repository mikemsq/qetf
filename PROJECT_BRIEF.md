## Project Overview

### What are we building?

I'm building a quantitative investment system that uses ETFs as investment instruments. 
The idea is to create and maintain a portfolio of several ETFs, rebalancing regularly, with the goal of exceeding S&P 500 returns. 

### Problem Statement

Create and maintain a portfolio of several ETFs, and generate rebalance instructions regularly. 

### Success Criteria

Exceed S&P 500 returns.
